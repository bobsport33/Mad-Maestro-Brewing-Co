Our Beers

Opus No. 8- Irish Red Ale

ABV%: 6.3%, IBU: 20, SRM: 8.4

Flavor Profile: This red ale follows the Irish tradition of getting the copper red color from a percentage of toasted barley. With the addition of some crystal malts, this beer has a nice, caramel/malty flavor.

Opus No. 7- Russian Imperial Stout

ABV%: 11.5%, IBU: 90, SRM: 26

Flavor Profile: Dark, rich, and bold. This is a big beer full of intese flavor. Dark biscuit flavor with hints of toffey and chocolate make up the malt profile in this stout.

Opus No. 10b- Belgian Saison

ABV%: 5.5%, IBU: 30, SRM: 4

Flavor Profile: A smooth wheat beer brewed with Belgian yeast strains. Subtle hints of corriander and clove balance out this refreshing unfiltered wheat beer.

Opus No. 29- Orange Milkshake I.P.A.

ABV%: 8.4%, IBU: 65, SRM: 6

Flavor Profile: This hazy beer is brusting with citrus flavors from the Mosaic, Citra, and El Dorado hop varities. Rounding out the hop flavors are orange zest, vanilla extract, and lactose. This beer is a dreamsicle in a bottle!

Opus No. 30- Belgian Dubbel

ABV%: 8%, IBU: 20, SRM: 12

Flavor Profile: This dark stone fruit forward beer has a rich, malty taste with a boozy finish.

Opus No. 32- Strawberry Milkshake I.P.A.

ABV%: 6%, IBU: 80, SRM: 5

Flavor Profile: Strawberry Sunday's anyone? This milkshake hazy I.P.A. has a very fruit forward flavor, being balanced with the additions of vanilla extract and lactose.

Locations

We have two locations that are open to the public. The first is our brewery, where we started making and distributing our beer. As we expanded our operations, it became necessary to expand and build a tap room that has additional brewing capacity.

Brewery

This location is located in Vernon Hills, Illinois. This is our original location that is still in production. This location is for tours only. Please make sure to sign up for an official tour before coming to this location.

Taproom

Our taproom is a more expansive location in the West Loop of Chicago, Illinois. There is a full service bar with all of our beers on tap, and a kitchen serving bbq. Although we conduct some of our brewring at this location, there are no tours of our taproom.

Schedule a Visit

Tours are held at our Vernon Hills, IL location. Tours are approximately 45 minutes long, which inculdes a tour of the facility, a look at the brewing process, and a tasting. Tours cannot be booked on site, please make sure to book your tour in advance.
